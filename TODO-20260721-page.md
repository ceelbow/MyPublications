## Design

- Style: scientific/academic — structured, serif headings, feels like a CV.
- Accent color: ocean blue.
- Header: name, "Post doc, Genome Sciences, University of Washington", and a
  short intro line drawn from ClaudeLab/docs/about-my-work.md (HAB research,
  metaproteomic/multi-omic time series).
- Stat cards: total papers, total citations, h-index.
- Charts: citations per year (bar/line), papers per year (bar).
- Publication list: sorted by year descending, title links to DOI.
- Dark-mode toggle.
- Data source: papers.json (all 11 works, preprints included, no exclusions).
